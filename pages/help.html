Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ticket?

You can book a ticket by selecting your route and clicking the "Search" button, then proceed to payment.

How do I pay?

We support M-Pesa payment. Click "Buy Now" after selecting your trip.

Can I cancel my ticket?

Yes, cancellations are allowed within a limited time before departure.

What cities do you cover?

Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Busia, Sirare and more.
