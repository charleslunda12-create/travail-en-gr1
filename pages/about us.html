Who We Are

Afri Nairobi Bus is a leading transport company based in Nairobi, Kenya. We provide reliable, safe, and affordable bus services connecting major cities such as Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Busia, and Sirare.

Our mission is to make travel easy and comfortable for everyone. Whether you are traveling for business or leisure, we ensure a smooth journey.
